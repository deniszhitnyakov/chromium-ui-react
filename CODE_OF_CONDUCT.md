# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good intent; ask before attributing bad intent.
- No personal attacks, no demographic-based insults, no off-topic promotion.
- Technical disagreement is welcome; personal conflict is not.

## Enforcement

If you see behavior that falls outside the standard linked above, or you want to report an incident confidentially, email **denis.zhitnyakov@gmail.com**. Reports are handled by the maintainers and are not made public without the reporter's consent.

The maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, ban accounts, and decline contributions that conflict with the Covenant — at their discretion and without prior notice.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: the GitHub repository, its issues and pull requests, the documentation site, and any other channel officially associated with the project.
